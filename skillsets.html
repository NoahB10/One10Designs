Skillsets & Equipment

The machines, tools, and expertise behind every One10 Designs creation

FDM 3D Printing

Equipped with a Prusa Core One, we produce high-quality FDM prints in PLA, PETG, ASA, and flexible filaments. Custom CAD design is available to tailor each project to your specific use case. Ideal for functional prototypes, custom enclosures, mechanical parts, and decorative pieces. Large build volume enables both detailed small prints and full-scale functional components.

Resin 3D Printing

Our resin printing setup delivers ultra-fine detail for miniatures, jewelry casting molds, and precision parts. Perfect for pieces that demand smooth surfaces and intricate geometry that FDM can't achieve — including wax-castable resins for lost-wax jewelry production.

Laser Cutting & Engraving

Precision laser cutting and engraving on wood and acrylic. From personalized engravings on custom backgammon boards to intricate cut patterns and detailed photo engravings — the laser adds a level of detail and personalization that sets each piece apart.

Woodworking Tools

A collection of handheld woodworking tools including a router, circular saws, jigsaws, and more — anything needed to make simple and elegant wood designs. Primarily working with olive wood and epoxy resin to create one-of-a-kind pieces.

About One10 Designs

One10 Designs is the creative workshop of Noah Bernten — a mechanical engineer with a passion for craftsmanship. Specializing in custom woodworking, 3D printing, and jewelry, every piece is thought out and designed by Noah alongside his tools and resources to deliver exactly what the client envisions.
