Torquise Epoxy Filled Olive Wood Mezuza

350 NIS

Pre-order 5 more to come; 1 Available

Dimensions:

HxWxT: 5.9x1.7x0.74 inches
Slot Size: 5 inches

Short Description:

This Mezuza began from a slab of olive wood. The shape of Israel was then cut out of the wood using a CNC Router which was programmed precisely. The piece was then placed in a custom mold to prepare for pouring in an epoxy resin. After much sanding and cleaning up the Shin was lasered onto the wood to complete the piece.

Message or email Noah to inquire more about custom ordering this style or purchasing this Mezuza.
